Summer support

Summer and September confidence.

The weeks before September can be a good time to steady things before the next school year starts.

Amanda can support children who are moving from Year 6 into Year 7, learners who need KS3 catch-up in maths or English, and pupils who have lost confidence after a difficult term. For some families, the concern is academic. For others, it is routine, independence, SEND needs or the worry that a new school year will feel too much too quickly.

As a tutor in Stockport, Amanda keeps the first step personal. Parents can talk through the year group, subject, confidence, SEND considerations and what school has already said before deciding whether tutoring is the right fit.

Useful for

  • Year 6 to Year 7 transition.
  • KS2 and KS3 maths or English catch-up.
  • Learners who need calm structure before September.
  • SEND-aware routine and confidence support.
  • Year 10 pupils who want to understand mock feedback before Year 11.
