Summer English

Summer English catch-up before September.

English can feel difficult for different reasons. Some children read the words but struggle to explain meaning. Some have ideas but cannot organise them on the page. Others worry about spelling, sentence structure, handwriting, vocabulary or getting started.

Amanda can use summer tutoring to make English feel more manageable before the next school year. Support can include reading fluency, comprehension, sentence confidence, paragraph structure, vocabulary, spelling, grammar and written answers.

For children moving from Year 6 to Year 7, English tutoring can help with the step into longer texts, fuller written answers and more independent work at secondary school.

For older learners, including pupils moving towards GCSE years, Amanda can help them look at how they read questions, organise ideas and explain answers clearly. This is careful confidence-building support, not a promise of a particular GCSE result.

Parents looking for an English tutor Stockport or GCSE English tutor Stockport can contact Amanda to talk through the year group, confidence and what school has said.
